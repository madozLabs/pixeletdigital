# Asset Production Plan

Status: Phase 1 planning baseline; implementation-neutral
Owner: Project owner
Scope: Pixel&Digital and Kwaliti Print live MVP worlds; Studio and Training receive teaser/foundation assets only.

## 1. Objective and guardrails

Produce a rights-cleared, accessible and performance-conscious asset set that explains, proves and converts. Authentic work, people, process, material and outcomes take priority over generic stock. Do not invent clients, claims, metrics, names, prices, dates, budgets, legal facts, colours, fonts or capabilities; exact visual identity choices remain owner decisions.

## 3. Asset brief fields

- Identifier, owning world, accountable owner, producer and approval status.
- Page/journey context, audience question, evidence or message supported and conversion role.
- Source/client/project, authenticity check and linked approved claim where relevant.
- Rights holder, consent/release, licence, credits, allowed channels, expiry and withdrawal path.
- Personal, confidential or location information and required privacy/legal approval.
- Composition needs for mobile and larger screens; safe crop and scale/context requirements.
- Alternative text, caption, transcript, audio description or decorative classification.
- Meaningful information that must survive no-motion, no-audio, no-3D and low-capability modes.
- Criticality, lightweight preview/fallback, stable presentation needs and editorial size/duration restraint.
- Review owner, blocking dependency, delivery state and archive constraint.

## 4. Production sequence

1. Freeze the approved page purpose, content claim and live-world scope before briefing.
2. Audit usable existing assets and record evidence, ownership, rights and quality gaps.
3. Prioritize navigation, primary message, service/capability explanation, proof and conversion support.
4. Brief short, typical and long/mobile-safe treatments; identify static equivalents before rich assets.
5. Capture or create only approved subjects and capabilities; record releases and credits at source.
6. Review accuracy, brand fit, mobile crops, accessible equivalents, performance risk and conversion value.
7. Approve, map usage contexts and archive superseded or withdrawn assets without destructive reuse.

## 5. Readiness gate

An asset is launch-ready only when ownership and rights are recorded, the represented claim/capability is verified, privacy/legal dependencies are cleared, mobile presentation preserves meaning, accessibility alternatives exist, enhanced media has a lightweight fallback, and the asset supports a defined content or conversion purpose. Missing final assets use labelled planning gaps, not fabricated public material.

The four Pixel&Digital PNG files under `assets/brand/pixel-digital/source/` are documented in `docs/01-brand/PIXEL_DIGITAL_LOGO_REGISTER.md` as repository source assets. They partially progress the logo audit but remain raster-only and do not pass the readiness gate: vector/master files, formal usage rules, rights authority, clear space, minimum size, typography and authoritative colour/gradient specifications remain pending. The sampled raster red `#E81B2C` is observational evidence only and is not an approved universal brand token.

## 6. Open owner decisions

- Final logos and usage rules, including authoritative Pixel&Digital vector/master files; approved colours, type families and photography direction.
- Approved clients, people, projects, testimonials and locations that may appear.
- Confirmed Kwaliti Print launch catalogue, materials, formats and capabilities.
- Studio and Training final names, identities and exact teaser wording; both remain foundation-only.
- Production budget and production method for photography, video, 3D and original sound.
- Launch languages/geographies, legal identity, consent/releases, notices and retention requirements.
- Launch date, which is not assumed and is required for production prioritisation.
