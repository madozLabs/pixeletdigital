# Content-to-Page Mapping Draft

Status: Provisional working draft; owner review required; not approved for publication
Scope: Documentation mapping only. It does not approve final navigation, URLs, copy, offers, claims or implementation.

## Mapping rule

The mapping uses only owner-stated services and capabilities from the reviewed drafts. Proposed taxonomy families are editorial aids, not commercial facts. Every page remains blocked until its facts, evidence, rights, accessibility, mobile presentation, conversion use and applicable owner decisions are approved.
