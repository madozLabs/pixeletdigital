# Owner Review Packet

Status: Action required from project owner

Use `Approve`, `Revise`, `Defer` or `Reject` for each decision. Add the preferred wording or correction when revising.

## Recommended review order

1. Launch status of each service and capability.
2. Editorial grouping and public naming.
3. Pricing and enquiry approach.
4. Languages and priority geography.
5. Evidence, clients and media rights.
6. Company, team, legal and privacy facts.
7. Studio and Training teaser wording.
