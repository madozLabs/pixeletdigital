# Launch Readiness Matrix

Status: Documentation working draft; owner review required; not approved for publication
Scope: Readiness assessment based only on owner-stated facts already recorded in the current content drafts. This matrix does not approve a service, capability, claim, offer, page or conversion route.

## Reading rule

- `Current/future status` records only the status present in the drafts. Every Pixel&Digital entry remains an owner-stated candidate, not an approved launch service.
- `Evidence status` and `Rights status` distinguish owner statements from publishable proof and permission. No approved client evidence, metric, testimonial or media rights are present in the reviewed drafts.
- `Content readiness` and `Conversion readiness` do not authorize publication or implementation.
- `Publication eligibility` remains blocked until the named owner decisions and item-level evidence, rights and content approvals are complete.
