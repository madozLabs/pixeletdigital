# CTO Recommendations for Owner Decisions

Status: Advisory only; no decision is approved by this document

## Use

These recommendations reduce avoidable scope and release risk. The project owner must still record the final disposition, wording/value, authority and date in `PHASE_1_OWNER_DECISION_FORM.md` and update `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`.

## Recommended owner review order

1. ODR-002 and ODR-003: freeze the actual launch offer.
2. ODR-004 to ODR-007: freeze conversion, pricing, languages and geography.
3. ODR-008 to ODR-011: preserve Studio and Training boundaries.
4. ODR-014, ODR-015, ODR-018, ODR-019 and ODR-022: establish evidence, rights and legal authority.
5. ODR-001, ODR-012 and ODR-013: finalize public positioning and identity authority.
6. ODR-016, ODR-017, ODR-020 and ODR-021: close production, release and measurement scope.

Phase 2 remains blocked until the owner completes the formal entry authorization in `PHASE_2_ENTRY_CRITERIA.md`.
