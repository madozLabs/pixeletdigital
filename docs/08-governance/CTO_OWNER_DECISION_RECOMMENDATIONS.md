# CTO Owner Decision Recommendations

Status: Advisory recommendations only; owner decision required

These recommendations support owner review of ODR-001 through ODR-022. They do not approve, close or change the status of any business or publication decision. The Owner Decision Register and completed Owner Decision Form remain the governing records for owner approval. Phase 2 architecture was authorized separately on 2026-07-15; production deployment and unresolved launch decisions remain separately gated.

## Guardrails

- Pixel&Digital services remain owner-stated candidates until explicitly approved for launch.
- Personalized Gadgets remains the only current-stated Kwaliti Print capability; this is not publication approval.
- Banner Printing, Vinyl Printing and 3D Lettering/CNC remain future-only.
- Studio and Training remain teaser/foundation only.
- No recommendation below supplies an unconfirmed fact, claim, price, date, legal term, identity rule, capability or metric.

## Recommendations

### ODR-001 — Official Pixel&Digital positioning and tagline

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before final content approval.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve one precise positioning statement and tagline, or explicitly defer the tagline while approving positioning; retain draft labels until then.
- **Rationale:** Positioning governs homepage hierarchy, service framing, SEO, claims and conversion copy.
- **Risk if deferred:** Final public copy and consistent conversion messaging cannot be approved.
- **Owner input still required:** Exact approved wording, whether a tagline is required, approver, date and authority/reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; `PROJECT_BIBLE.md`; Pixel&Digital brand and content drafts.

### ODR-002 — Pixel&Digital launch services

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide at scope freeze.
- **Concise recommendation:** Explicitly approve, defer or reject each of the eight owner-stated candidate services and approve the exact public name of every retained launch service.
- **Rationale:** All eight services are candidates, not approved launch offers; item-level decisions prevent inferred bundles or capabilities.
- **Risk if deferred:** Navigation, service pages, forms, evidence requirements and content scope remain unfrozen.
- **Owner input still required:** Per-service disposition, exact public names, factual boundaries, approver, date and evidence/reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; `CONFIRMED_OWNER_FACTS.md`; Pixel&Digital catalogue, taxonomy, page mapping and readiness drafts.

### ODR-003 — Kwaliti Print launch scope

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide at scope freeze, preserving current/future boundaries.
- **Concise recommendation:** Evaluate Personalized Gadgets as the sole current-stated launch candidate and keep Banner Printing, Vinyl Printing and 3D Lettering/CNC deferred as future-only.
- **Rationale:** This reflects the only confirmed current/future distinction without turning current-stated status into publication approval.
- **Risk if deferred:** Kwaliti Print discovery, quotation qualification, proof requirements and page scope remain blocked; future capabilities could be misrepresented.
- **Owner input still required:** Launch disposition for Personalized Gadgets, exact approved operational scope and public naming, approver, date and evidence/reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; `CONFIRMED_OWNER_FACTS.md`; Kwaliti Print catalogue, taxonomy, page mapping and readiness drafts.

### ODR-004 — Pricing visibility

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before final UX/content approval.
- **Concise recommendation:** Choose explicitly between publishing approved pricing information and a quotation-only presentation; publish no price until approved.
- **Rationale:** Pricing treatment changes catalogue copy, qualification, forms and conversion expectations.
- **Risk if deferred:** Catalogue and quotation journeys cannot be finalized without risking invented or misleading commercial information.
- **Owner input still required:** Chosen visibility policy, approved wording or values if applicable, scope of application, approver, date and authority/reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; catalogue, page mapping, journey and wireframe documents.

### ODR-005 — Consultation and contact approach

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before final UX and release planning.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve the launch conversion routes and consultation/contact policy at a provider-neutral level before any implementation choice.
- **Rationale:** Calls to action, journey steps, requested data and privacy dependencies require a single governed policy.

### ODR-012 — Final logos and usage rules

- **Recommended disposition:** Complete before visual design and asset production approval.
- **Concise recommendation:** Obtain authoritative master assets, rights authority and formal usage rules; treat registered raster sources only as evidence, not a complete approved identity system.
- **Rationale:** Existing raster files do not establish vector masters, rights, variants, clear space, minimum size, typography or authoritative colour/gradient rules.
- **Risk if deferred:** Asset production and consistent, authorized brand use remain blocked.
- **Owner input still required:** Authoritative assets, rights authority, approved variants and usage rules, approver, date and references.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; logo register and brand bibles/foundations.

### ODR-013 — Colours, gradients and type families

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before visual design and accessibility approval.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve an authoritative colour/gradient and typography specification through the accountable brand authority; retain only directional intent until then.
- **Rationale:** Observed raster colour is evidence, not an approved token, and no exact fonts are approved.
- **Risk if deferred:** Consistent visual production and meaningful contrast/accessibility approval cannot be completed.
- **Owner input still required:** Exact authoritative specifications, usage scope, source/authority, licensing or rights confirmation where applicable, approver and date.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; brand bibles, visual foundations and art-direction brief.

### ODR-014 — Clients, cases, metrics and testimonials

- **Recommended disposition:** Approve only evidence-backed, permissioned items; otherwise defer them from MVP publication.
- **Concise recommendation:** Review each proposed evidence item individually for accuracy, permission, attribution and approved wording before inclusion.
- **Rationale:** No client, case study, testimonial, metric or attributable outcome is currently approved.
- **Risk if deferred:** Credibility content remains unavailable; publishing without approval creates claim and rights risk.
- **Owner input still required:** Exact items, supporting evidence, permissions, attribution, approved claims/wording, approver and date.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; evidence framework, content inventory and readiness drafts.

### ODR-015 — Media subjects, rights and production direction

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide a minimum rights-cleared production scope before commissioning or publication.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve subjects and production direction only after documenting ownership, consent, licences and permitted uses; otherwise use no unapproved media.
- **Rationale:** Media ambition must remain subordinate to verified rights and launch content needs.
- **Risk if deferred:** Asset production and release media remain blocked; ungoverned commissioning could waste budget or create rights exposure.
- **Owner input still required:** Approved subjects, intended uses, rights/consent evidence, production direction, accountable reviewer, approver and date.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; asset production plan, content inventory and readiness drafts.

### ODR-016 — Media-production budget

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before production commitment; retain essential/static-first planning meanwhile.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve a budget ceiling and priorities only after the minimum asset scope and rights requirements are clear.
- **Rationale:** Spend should follow an approved production need rather than define unsupported ambition.
- **Risk if deferred:** Production scope and schedule cannot be committed, although no-spend documentation planning can continue.
- **Owner input still required:** Approved budget, inclusions/exclusions, spending authority, priority order, approver, date and reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; asset production plan and roadmap.

### ODR-017 — Launch date

- **Recommended disposition:** Defer commitment until blocking Phase 1 decisions have accountable resolution plans.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve no public or internal launch commitment until scope, content, evidence, legal/privacy and conversion dependencies support a credible date.
- **Rationale:** A date chosen before readiness evidence would create schedule pressure without resolving launch blockers.
- **Risk if deferred:** Sequencing lacks a fixed endpoint, but premature commitment carries greater delivery and quality risk.
- **Owner input still required:** Target date or explicit deferral, business constraints, acceptance authority, approver, date of decision and reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; roadmap and launch-readiness matrix.

### ODR-018 — Legal identity and public notices

- **Recommended disposition:** Provide and validate before final content and release.
- **Concise recommendation:** Supply authoritative legal identity data and required notices, with an accountable legal reviewer; draft no substitute facts.
- **Rationale:** Footer, forms, ownership statements and compliance content require authoritative legal inputs.
- **Risk if deferred:** Final public pages and forms cannot be approved for release.
- **Owner input still required:** Applicable legal identity and notice information, jurisdiction-relevant requirements, accountable reviewer, authority/reference, approver and date.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; company information intake, content inventory and readiness drafts.

### ODR-019 — Privacy, consent and retention

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before final forms, analytics and release.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve purpose-specific privacy and consent wording, minimum data handling and retention rules through an accountable reviewer.
- **Rationale:** Forms, leads, media and analytics cannot be governed solely by a general intent to minimize data.
- **Risk if deferred:** Conversion forms, operational handling and analytics remain blocked or legally exposed.
- **Owner input still required:** Data purposes, fields, lawful/authorized handling basis as applicable, consent wording, retention/deletion rules, accountable reviewer, approver, date and reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; journeys, wireframes, roles/permissions and readiness drafts.

### ODR-020 — Analytics scope and provider constraints

- **Recommended disposition:** Define business measurement needs in Phase 1; defer provider selection to authorized Phase 2 planning.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve only meaningful event objectives, privacy constraints and provider constraints now; select no provider through this recommendation.
- **Rationale:** Measurement intent affects consent and readiness. Phase 2 architecture is authorized, but analytics-provider commitment and production deployment remain outside the approved scope.
- **Risk if deferred:** Conversion evidence requirements and privacy planning remain vague; provider work must not begin.
- **Owner input still required:** Approved measurement objectives/events, prohibited or required constraints, privacy expectations, budget/provider authority if later applicable, approver and date.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; MVP scope, project bible and Phase 2 entry criteria.

### ODR-021 — Insights in MVP

- **Recommended disposition:** Exclude from MVP unless a governed content inventory is demonstrably sufficient and approved.
- **Concise recommendation:** Require an explicit inventory and accountable editorial capacity before approving Insights; otherwise defer it.
- **Rationale:** An empty or weak editorial area adds navigation and maintenance cost without established conversion value.
- **Risk if deferred:** No critical MVP capability is lost; including it without governed content creates stale or low-quality publication risk.
- **Owner input still required:** Chosen inclusion decision, approved content inventory, editorial owner, publication cadence/maintenance commitment if included, approver, date and reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; MVP scope, information architecture, content inventory and page mapping.

### ODR-022 — Kwaliti Print file acceptance and handling

- **Recommended disposition:** Decide before final quotation journey and release.
- **Concise recommendation:** Approve customer-facing file guidance only after operations and privacy handling are verified; promise no formats, limits or handling behavior beforehand.
- **Rationale:** No accepted formats or handling rules are confirmed, and file intake affects mobile usability, privacy and operations.
- **Risk if deferred:** File-upload or file-submission guidance and the final quotation journey remain blocked.
- **Owner input still required:** Whether files are accepted, approved formats and limits if any, submission route, handling/retention/deletion rules, operational owner, approver, date and reference.
- **Downstream docs affected:** `OWNER_DECISION_REGISTER.md`; Kwaliti Print catalogue, journeys, wireframes and review packet.

## Advisory conclusion

Owner resolution should continue for launch scope and public facts, conversion policy, brand authority, evidence/rights, and legal/privacy inputs. Deferrals recommended above preserve the approved MVP boundary; they do not close the corresponding ODR items. Phase 2 architecture is authorized, while public release, production deployment and unapproved provider commitments remain blocked.
