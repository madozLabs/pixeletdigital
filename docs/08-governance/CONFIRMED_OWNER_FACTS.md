## Confirmed facts

### Pixel&Digital

- Pixel&Digital is the parent agency brand.
- The owner has stated the following service candidates: Social Media Management for Facebook, TikTok, Instagram and LinkedIn; Website Creation; Facebook Advertising; Graphic Design; Motion Design; Video Editing; Content Creation; Audiovisual Production.
- These are candidate services only. Their final launch inclusion, public names, scope, evidence and commercial treatment are not yet approved.
- An existing photo/video studio supports audiovisual activity; its final separate name and identity remain unresolved.

### Kwaliti Print

- Kwaliti Print is a distinct print and fabrication unit within the Pixel&Digital ecosystem.
- Personalized Gadgets is the only capability currently stated as available.
- Banner Printing is future-only.
- Vinyl Printing is future-only.
- 3D Lettering using CNC is future-only.
- No future capability may be presented as currently available or receive a capability-specific quotation route before explicit launch approval.

### Brand assets

- Four Pixel&Digital PNG raster source assets are versioned in the repository.
- The observed raster red is approximately `#E81B2C`; it is not an approved universal brand token.
- Vector masters, formal logo rules, typography, authoritative colours/gradients and rights authority remain unresolved.

### Scope boundaries

- Pixel&Digital and Kwaliti Print are the live MVP worlds.
- Studio and Training remain teaser/foundation only unless the owner explicitly changes scope.
- Architecture technical and application development remain unauthorized until explicit owner approval.

## Change rule

A new statement is added here only when explicitly confirmed by the owner. Editorial groupings, inferred capabilities and planning defaults must remain in draft documents, not in this factual record.
