## 4. Recommended low-risk posture

Until the owner records a different approved answer:

- use quotation-only commercial language;
- use simple intent-based contact routes, without automated booking;
- exclude Insights from MVP;
- keep Studio and Training out of full launch scope;
- keep future Kwaliti Print capabilities out of current capability pages and quotation routes;
- plan essential, static-first media without assuming production spend;
- select no analytics provider before privacy and Phase 2 architecture decisions.

These are CTO recommendations and planning controls, not owner approvals.
