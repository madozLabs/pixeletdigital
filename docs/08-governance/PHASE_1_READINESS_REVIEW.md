# Phase 1 Readiness Review

Status: Working assessment; owner review required
Assessment basis: repository state, confirmed owner facts, content drafts, brand/UX foundations and open decision register.

## 1. Executive verdict

Phase 1 is structurally advanced but not complete. The project has a governed vision, brand architecture, UX journeys, wireframe specifications, content protocols, logo evidence, owner-stated service drafts and launch-readiness tracking.

Phase 1 cannot close yet because the launch offer, public facts, evidence, legal/privacy inputs and conversion policy remain unapproved. Architecture and development remain blocked.

## 2. Completed foundations

- Project vision, MVP boundary and roadmap are documented.
- Pixel&Digital and Kwaliti Print are the live MVP worlds.
- Studio and Training remain teaser/foundation only.
- Public and administrative journeys, information architecture and wireframe specifications exist.
- Roles, permissions, content ownership and evidence standards exist.
- Four Pixel&Digital raster logo sources are versioned and registered.
- Provisional visual foundations and art direction exist.
- Governed content-intake templates and owner-stated catalogue drafts exist.
- Current versus future Kwaliti Print capability status is explicitly recorded.
## 3. Blocking gaps

- ODR-001: final positioning and tagline.
- ODR-002: approved Pixel&Digital launch-service catalogue.
- ODR-003: approved Kwaliti Print current capability catalogue.
- ODR-004 to ODR-007: pricing, consultation, languages and geography.
- ODR-008 to ODR-011: Studio and Training names, identities and teaser boundaries.
- ODR-012 and ODR-013: authoritative logo system, colours and typography.
- ODR-014 and ODR-015: approved evidence, testimonials, media and rights.
- ODR-018 and ODR-019: legal identity, notices, privacy, consent and retention.
- ODR-022: Kwaliti Print file-acceptance guidance.

## 4. Current publication readiness

No service or capability is currently eligible for publication as an approved launch offer.

Personalized Gadgets is the only Kwaliti Print capability currently stated as available, but its operational scope, evidence, rights, quotation route and publication approvals are incomplete.

Banner Printing, Vinyl Printing and 3D Lettering/CNC are future-only and must not receive current-capability pages or quotation routes.

## 5. Phase 1 close condition

Phase 1 may close only when the owner has resolved the minimum launch decisions, every retained launch item has an accountable owner and approved factual scope, critical legal/privacy inputs are available, and the content/page model can be frozen without invented claims.
