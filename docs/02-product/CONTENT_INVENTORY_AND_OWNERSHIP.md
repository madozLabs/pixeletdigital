# Content Inventory and Ownership

Status: Phase 1 working baseline; implementation-neutral
Owner: Project owner
Scope: Pixel&Digital and Kwaliti Print are live MVP worlds; Studio and Training are teaser/foundation only.

## 1. Purpose and rules

Use this inventory to expose every launch content dependency, accountable role and release gate. Do not fill gaps with invented clients, claims, metrics, names, prices, dates, budgets, legal facts, colours, fonts or capabilities.

- One accountable content owner approves accuracy and freshness for each item.
- The owning business unit controls routine edits; cross-unit content names one lead owner and all contributors.
- Editorial approval does not replace evidence, rights, brand, accessibility or legal review.
- Readiness states are `Missing`, `Draft`, `In review`, `Approved`, `Published` and `Archived`.
- `Approved` means source, evidence, rights, mobile presentation, accessibility and conversion use have been checked.

## 3. Required fields for every inventory record

- Identifier, content type, owning world, accountable owner and contributors.
- Intended audience, page or usage context, primary message and conversion action.
- Source, evidence reference, fact-check date and approver.
- Media owner, usage rights, credit, permitted contexts and archive constraints.
- Alternative text, caption, transcript or decorative classification as appropriate.
- Mobile short/typical/long copy and reading order; no hover-only dependency.
- Critical content priority, lightweight fallback and performance risk.
- Personal-data presence, consent or notice dependency, retention dependency and access scope.
- Readiness state, blocking gap, next action, next owner and review trigger.

## 4. Readiness review

Before publication, the accountable owner verifies that the item is accurate, attributable, rights-cleared, understandable on mobile, accessible without enhanced media, performance-bounded and connected to one appropriate next action. Legal/privacy-dependent copy remains blocked until the owner supplies approved identity, notices, consent wording and retention rules.

## 5. Open owner decisions

- Official positioning, taglines, launch services and confirmed Kwaliti Print categories/capabilities.
- Launch languages, priority geographies and pricing visibility.
- Consultation route and approved form next-step wording.
- Approved evidence, testimonials, client attribution and available media.
- Studio and Training final names and teaser wording; neither is a live MVP world.
- Legal identity, public notices, consent wording, privacy terms and retention requirements.
- Whether Insights has enough governed content for MVP inclusion.
- Media-production budget, launch date and final brand assets, colours and type families.
