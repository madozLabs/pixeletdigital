# Pixel&Digital — Content Collection Protocol

Status: Operational Phase 1 protocol

## 1. Rule

Collect facts and source material before writing final public copy. Unknown information stays marked as a gap; it is never replaced by a plausible invention.

## 2. Submission record

Every submission records: content type, owning world, accountable owner, source, date received, evidence, rights status, privacy/legal dependency, readiness, next action and reviewer.

## 3. Pixel&Digital services

For each proposed launch service collect: approved name, client problem, confirmed scope, exclusions, delivery approach, required inputs, typical outputs, related proof, enquiry route, owner and review cadence.

## 4. Kwaliti Print capabilities

For each capability collect: approved category, application, confirmed materials/formats, constraints, quantity or dimension questions, file guidance, service area, fulfilment limitations, authentic examples, operational owner and quotation route. Never infer inventory, turnaround, price or feasibility.

## 5. Team

Collect approved public name, role, concise biography, portrait, consent, image rights, contact exposure decision and review trigger. Do not publish personal data beyond the approved purpose.

## 6. Projects and case studies

Collect client disclosure level, context, challenge, scope, contributions by business unit, deliverables, dated results, source records, limitations, approved testimonial, media, rights and confidentiality review.

## 7. Testimonials

Require exact wording, speaker identity or approved anonymity level, role/context, permission, approved channels and review date. Never paraphrase text as a direct quote.

## 8. Legal and operational facts

Collect official legal identity, address/contact facts approved for publication, required notices, privacy terms, consent wording, retention rules, upload notices and responsible reviewer. Draft placeholders cannot pass the release gate.

## 9. Media and rights

For each asset record creator, owner, licence or assignment, subject/client release, permitted use, restrictions, expiry, credit, alternative text/caption/transcript, mobile crop and lightweight fallback.

## 10. Readiness

A record is `Approved` only when accuracy, evidence, rights, privacy/legal dependencies, mobile presentation, accessibility and conversion use are reviewed. Studio and Training content remains teaser/foundation only unless an explicit scope change is approved.

## 11. Collection order

1. Official company and contact facts.
2. Final launch services and Kwaliti Print capabilities.
3. Approved team and ownership.
4. Evidence candidates and permissions.
5. Form questions and operational next steps.
6. Media audit and production gaps.
7. Final copy and visual production.
